Ben Toews MastahYeti

I'm Ben Toews (aka mastahyeti). Welcome to my site. I am a security consultant and resesarcher for Neohapsis. I work mostly on network and application penetration testing. I do a lot of research and try to publish as much of it as possible. On this site, you will find some of my papers as well as links to some of my tools.

As of late, I have been working on new SQL injection tool called BBQSQL. I have also been busy writing and speaking. A whitepaper I wrote regarding a tool of mine called httpShell was recently published in HITB Magazine and I will be talking on the same subject at Thotcon 0x03. You can also check out some of my research on Neohapsis Labs.

When I'm not busy hacking, I am an avid enjoyer of beer and pizza. I also homebrew, cycle, and make music.

Enjoy the site.
